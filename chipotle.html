The best go-to food

When people ask the question, "if you were stranded on an island with only one meal what would it be?" My go-to answer is always Chipotle. Although I like other restaurants better, specifically quality sit down places, Chipotle is always a true and constant. It always fills me up and has the best flavor combination. I can never get sick of it! And for that, it is the best.
